Site name:

Rose City Chamber of Commerce

Purpose

WE care about Local companies of Portland. Portland is one the places in the states that provide a home for all. The diversity of Portland is incredible and we stand together as one because this is our home. We all come together to make Portland's culture what it is! This is the meeting point for all small businesses and all that want to support!

Scenarios

Scenario 1

Business owner needs some help and has questions about the site. The business owner can find a customer support phone number and email.

Scenario 2

Business owner can search for others in the same industry by going to the site and getting contact information to connect.

Scenario 3

The city has many festivals and events, so they can figure out the time, location, and businesses attending.
